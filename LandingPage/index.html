Help Indeed

One day, In a deep forest, All alone,
When the world felt heavy, and I didn't know what to do,
I heard a beautiful butterfly say,"I am here for you."
She said she is not good at comfort with a humble, gentle heart,
But she gave me the very words I needed for a life.
She didn't tell me "Stop it" or "You must be strong"
She just softly whispered "You aren't gonna lose"
And in those simple promising words,I found a will to fight,
She made my heavy darkness feel a little bit more light.
"Just cry" she said, made my eyes red,
So I let the tears fall,like a sparky cleansing, summer rain,
I felt the sharp edges of my sorrow and my pain.
She is simple,honest comfort and chased away my fear.
So thank you for your patience,and thank you for the "cry"
You're the very best comfort,and I'm so glad you're my ally.
